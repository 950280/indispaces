  • The shrine was rediscovered about 700 years ago by a saint, and since then, it has been a major pilgrimage center.
  • What attracts tourists most is the spiritual aura of the cave, the 12 km trek from Katra to Bhawan, and the scenic Himalayan views along the way.
  • Modern facilities like battery cars, ponies, palkis, and a ropeway (to Bhairon Temple) make the journey comfortable for all age groups.

  • Banganga & Charan Paduka ‚Äì Important points on the trekking route with mythological significance.
  • Katra Town ‚Äì Local markets for dry fruits, woollens, and religious souvenirs.

  • Rajma Chawal ‚Äì Iconic J&K dish.
